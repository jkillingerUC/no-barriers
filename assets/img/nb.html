No Barriers

The UChicago Commitment

No Barriers is a comprehensive plan to increase access to college, support students as they receive an empowering education, and prepare them for lifelong professional success, so students can focus on what is truly important — their education.

Access

There is no application fee for students who apply for need-based financial aid at the University of Chicago. UChicago admissions counselors will be hosting free seminars and workshops on college admissions and financial aid across the country.

Affordability

UChicago meets 100% of every family’s demonstrated need through our need-based financial aid. Grant-based financial aid means there are no loans and students will be graduating from UChicago debt-free. No student debt means graduates will leave the College free to explore, experiment, pursue their passions, and begin their adult lives on solid footing without debt influencing their future plans.

Outcomes

No Barriers adds a spectrum of new resources to the College’s strong career advising. From day one, every student receives comprehensive career support and an individualized plan, with funded internships, international treks, and industry-specific advising to assist students of all majors and backgrounds plan their next steps. College is about discovering your passion and learning the steps necessary to follow it, and we are here to support you..

Empowering Education

Education for All

A University of Chicago education is creative, open-minded, intellectually stimulating and transformative. No Barriers fulfills UChicago’s promise to graduate students who are debt-free and prepared for life-long success, regardless of their chosen major or background before coming to the College.

The UChicago Difference

The University of Chicago is a leading community of creative, motivated, and inspired scholars who collaborate to enrich human life through their work. UChicago’s Core Curriculum gives each undergraduate a comprehensive liberal arts education rooted in discussion-style learning and debate; paired with one of our over 50 majors, students leave the College with fundamental skills and specialized knowledge in their chosen area. Critical thinking and interpersonal skills are emphasized, making this education adaptable to changing industries and careers that bridge fields. By making this education affordable for all through loan-free financial aid, we are committed to ensuring every UChicago undergraduate benefits from this educational style and is shown an unobstructed path to follow their passions.

I consider my education a gift and I hope to give this gift to future UChicago students."

Enriching Human Life

Knowledge to last a lifetime

The College seeks students who are intellectual explorers, and prepares them to be leaders in their chosen fields. Career advising and mentored internship programs help students find ways to apply their intellectual experiences to enrich a career or professional field. No Barriers will make these programs even more effective by giving students additional resources to help pursue their interests and passions.

Professional Development

New resources include special career advising for first-year students, early job shadowing externships, and specialized advising in a variety of career and professional paths through the “UChicago Careers In” programs. Workshops empower all students to become effective leaders in both the classroom and the workplace. 1,000 fully-paid Metcalf Internships let students explore a variety of career and professional paths, especially in new cities and countries. Treks across the country and around the world are opportunities for students to meet with employers in their ideal field and see all kinds of careers in action.

Expanded Odyssey Scholarships

UChicago’s Odyssey Scholarships support low and middle-income students from across the country. Students do not have summer and term-time earning expectations, and every Odyssey Scholar will be guaranteed a summer internship or research opportunity, meaning summers are yours to explore. Pre-orientation programming focused on personal finance, self-advocacy, and accessing university resources, and cross-year mentoring and alumni engagement helps students from low-income backgrounds enjoy expanded opportunities. Preparing talented students to leave the University confident, knowledgeable, and poised to succeed is what makes No Barriers revolutionary.
